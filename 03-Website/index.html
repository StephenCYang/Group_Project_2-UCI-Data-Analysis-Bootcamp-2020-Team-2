Data analysis tools for your fantasy premier team.

Intro

Looking for that underrated forward? Who is the best midfielder I can afford? Are expensive goalkeepers really worth it? The data can tell you.

This site pulls data from the Fantasy premier league and updates is in real time. Gain information on players by their position, goals, assists, bonus points, form, etc. Use the postion tab to find out who are the best forwards, midfielders, defenders and goalkeepers for your team.
